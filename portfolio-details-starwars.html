Star Wars The Clone Wars: Special Ops

Project information

  • Category: Level Design Prototype
  • Project date: May 2022

Star Wars The Clone Wars: Special Ops is a Level Design prototype for a Star Wars 3D platformer video game. This is the prototype of the game tutorial where basic movement and mechanics such as jumping, climbing, and other parkour elements are taught to the player.

This project was made in Unreal Engine 4 by me and a partner for the "Level Design" subject at CITM, UPC. We used free assets from the UE Marketplace to create the interior of a spaceship and we developed the basics mechanics but we put special focus on the player guidance. Player Guidance elements such as motion, lights pointing the way, ground objects making a path, etc, are all over the level.
